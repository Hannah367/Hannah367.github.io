Hannah Stutely - Portfolio

I am in my final year at the University of York, studying Interactive Media. I want to build a career in user experience and interface design.

Here are my highlights, from uni, my spare time, and from when I was younger and got bitten by the digital bug. I have developed a wide range of relevant skills from these projects. You can find out more about me, or use the menus above to see all of my projects.

User Centered Design

My final year project is an app based around female safety on nights out. As well as UI design, this developed my core UCD skills through research, affinity maps, personas, and user journey maps.

Critical Design

As part of my critical design module, I created a design fiction based around the future of health implants and supplements.

UXD Exhibit

During second year I created a project in Adobe XD to showcase an interactive exhibit for the National Railway Museum in York.

Game Design

At the end of second year I took on the Team Leader role in our group project to create the game Party Animals: Hospital Rush.

Web Development

I created a website to score and analyse the results of cricket matches. Built using JS, WebSQL, HTML and CSS.

NFTs

In my spare time I decided to create my own set of NFTs each with a unique story. This helped me learn about potential future uses of my design and storytelling skills.
